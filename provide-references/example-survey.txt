## Survey Summary:  Q3 Customer Feedback

### Top Pain Points
- **Performance Issues**: 45% of respondents reported slow load times during peak hours
- **Navigation Concerns**: 32% found the menu structure confusing for new users
- **Limited Customization**: 28% wanted more control over dashboard layout

### Feature Requests
- **Integration with CRM tools** (requested by 12 respondents)
- **Enhanced mobile notifications** (requested by 8 respondents)
- **Custom report builder** (requested by 15 respondents)

### Sentiment Analysis
- Overall satisfaction: 7.2/10
- Most loved feature: Real-time collaboration (mentioned 23 times)
- Most requested improvement: Performance optimization
